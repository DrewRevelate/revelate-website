Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our company and approach

Revelate Operations was founded by Drew Lambert and Melanie Tummino, who saw a critical gap in how businesses were utilizing their data. With complementary expertise in Salesforce implementation and data analytics, they joined forces to create a consultancy that helps companies transform their data into strategic assets that drive growth and operational excellence.

Our unique strength lies in our dual expertise in both technical implementation and strategic business analysis. We don't just configure systems; we align technology with business processes and strategic objectives. Our approach focuses on tangible results, knowledge transfer, and long-term partnership rather than creating dependencies on our services.

While we specialize in SaaS and technology companies, our methodologies and solutions can be applied across various industries. We've successfully worked with clients in healthcare, financial services, professional services, manufacturing, and non-profit sectors. Our approach is adaptable to any organization looking to better leverage their data for growth.

The length of our engagements varies based on the scope and complexity of the project. Initial implementations or transformations typically range from 2-6 months, while ongoing strategic partnerships can extend for years. We focus on delivering quick wins within the first 30 days while working toward more comprehensive long-term solutions.

We work with businesses of all sizes, from startups to enterprise organizations. Our solutions are scalable and can be tailored to fit your specific needs and budget. We believe that companies at any stage can benefit from data-driven operations and thoughtful technology implementation.

We establish clear, measurable KPIs at the beginning of every engagement. These typically include metrics like revenue growth, process efficiency improvements, user adoption rates, reduction in manual effort, and return on investment. We regularly report on these metrics throughout our engagement to ensure we're delivering tangible value.
