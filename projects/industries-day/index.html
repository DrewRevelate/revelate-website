About This Presentation

This presentation was created for undergraduate business students at UMass Lowell as part of Industries Day 2025. It covers the rapidly evolving landscape of Revenue Operations (RevOps) and how automation is transforming the sales process in today's high-speed business environment.

Revenue Operations Sales Automation CRM Systems Sales Technology Business Education

Key Topics Covered

  • The evolution of sales technology from analog to digital systems
  • How RevOps unifies sales, marketing, and customer success
  • Current automation solutions transforming the sales landscape
  • The balance between human expertise and automated systems
  • The future of sales technology and what it means for business professionals
