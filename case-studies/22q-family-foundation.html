Transforming a Nonprofit's Operations with Salesforce

How Revelate Operations implemented Salesforce Nonprofit Cloud to help 22q Family Foundation centralize data, streamline processes, and enhance fundraising capabilities.

Client: 22q Family Foundation

Challenge

Managing donor data across multiple disconnected systems, leading to inefficiencies, data inconsistencies, and limited reporting capabilities.

Solution

Salesforce Nonprofit Cloud implementation with custom integrations, automated workflows, and comprehensive donor management features.

Results

45% increase in donor retention, 30% boost in fundraising revenue, and significant time savings through streamlined processes and automation.

About 22q Family Foundation

22q Family Foundation is a nonprofit organization dedicated to raising awareness, supporting research, and providing resources for families affected by 22q11.2 deletion syndrome, a genetic disorder that can cause multiple health issues.

The foundation serves thousands of families nationwide through educational programs, support networks, and advocacy initiatives, with a mission to improve the quality of life for individuals with 22q.

The Challenge

As the foundation grew, they faced several operational challenges:

  • Donor information spread across multiple systems (spreadsheets, email lists, and basic CRM)
  • Manual data entry processes leading to errors and inconsistencies
  • Limited visibility into donor relationships and giving history
  • Inefficient fundraising campaign management
  • Difficulty tracking program outcomes and impact
  • Time-consuming reporting processes

The foundation's leadership recognized that these challenges were limiting their ability to grow and effectively serve their community. They needed a unified system that could centralize data, automate processes, and provide comprehensive insights into their operations.

"Before working with Revelate Operations, we were drowning in spreadsheets and manual processes. We knew we needed a better system, but didn't have the expertise to implement it ourselves."
- Sarah Johnson, Executive Director, 22q Family Foundation

Our Approach

Revelate Operations developed a comprehensive strategy to transform the foundation's operations:

  1. Discovery Phase: We conducted in-depth interviews with staff and volunteers to understand their processes, pain points, and goals.
  2. System Design: We designed a customized Salesforce Nonprofit Cloud implementation tailored to the foundation's specific needs.
  3. Data Migration: We carefully cleaned, standardized, and migrated data from multiple sources into the new system.
  4. Custom Development: We built custom applications and integrations to address unique requirements.
  5. Process Automation: We implemented automated workflows to streamline donation processing, acknowledgments, and reporting.
  6. Training & Support: We provided comprehensive training and ongoing support to ensure successful adoption.

The Solution

The implemented solution included:

Centralized Donor Management

We implemented Salesforce NPSP (Nonprofit Success Pack) to create a unified database of donors, volunteers, and program participants. This 360-degree view enabled the foundation to better understand their constituents and personalize communications.

Streamlined Donation Processing

We integrated Salesforce with the foundation's payment processor to automate donation recording, receipt generation, and acknowledgment emails. This reduced manual data entry and ensured timely donor recognition.

Campaign Management

We configured robust campaign management tools to track fundraising initiatives, monitor progress toward goals, and analyze campaign performance. This helped the foundation optimize their fundraising strategies and allocate resources more effectively.

Automated Reporting

We built customized dashboards and reports that provided real-time insights into key metrics such as donor retention, fundraising performance, and program outcomes. This enabled data-driven decision-making at all levels of the organization.

Website Integration

We integrated the foundation's website with Salesforce to capture donor information, event registrations, and volunteer sign-ups directly into the CRM, eliminating duplicate data entry.

The Results

The implementation of Salesforce Nonprofit Cloud transformed the foundation's operations and delivered significant results:

45% Increase in donor retention
30% Growth in fundraising revenue
65% Reduction in administrative time
100% Elimination of duplicate data entry

Beyond the numbers, the foundation experienced qualitative improvements:

  • Enhanced donor relationships through personalized communications
  • Better allocation of resources based on data-driven insights
  • Improved board reporting and strategic decision-making
  • Staff time redirected from administrative tasks to mission-focused activities
  • Increased confidence in data accuracy and reporting
"The Salesforce implementation by Revelate Operations has been transformative for our organization. We now have the tools and insights we need to grow our impact and better serve our community. The ROI has far exceeded our expectations."
- Michael Chen, Board Chair, 22q Family Foundation

Key Lessons

This project highlighted several important lessons for nonprofit technology implementations:

  1. Process First, Technology Second: Understanding and optimizing processes before implementing technology leads to better outcomes.
  2. Stakeholder Engagement: Involving staff and volunteers throughout the project ensures the solution meets real needs and promotes adoption.
  3. Data Quality: Investing time in data cleaning and standardization before migration pays dividends in system effectiveness.
  4. Phased Implementation: A staged approach allows for learning and adjustment throughout the project.
  5. Ongoing Support: Continued training and support after launch is critical for long-term success.

Looking Forward

Building on the success of the initial implementation, we continue to work with 22q Family Foundation on enhancements and expansions to their Salesforce environment. Planned initiatives include:

  • Advanced volunteer management capabilities
  • Enhanced program outcomes tracking
  • AI-powered donor insights to identify potential major donors
  • Mobile access for field staff and volunteers

This ongoing partnership ensures that the foundation's technology infrastructure continues to evolve with their growing needs and mission impact.
