Our Story

Revelate Operations was founded in 2021 by Drew Lambert and Melanie Tummino, who recognized a critical gap in the SaaS consulting landscape. Both had extensive experience working with SaaS companies and witnessed firsthand how many organizations were struggling to harness the full potential of their data.

Having worked on opposite sides of the revenue operations spectrum – Drew in sales operations and Melanie in data analytics – they realized that the most powerful solutions came from bridging these worlds. They founded Revelate with a clear mission: to help SaaS companies transform their raw data into strategic wealth.

What began as a two-person consultancy has grown into a team of specialists who bring deep expertise across the entire revenue operations spectrum. Today, we're proud to partner with clients ranging from high-growth startups to established enterprises, helping them unlock the full potential of their data assets.
